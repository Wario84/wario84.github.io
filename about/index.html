About

I'm a PhD candidate of the Programme on Innovation, Economics and Governance for Development (IEGD) at UNU-MERIT. I'm passionate about data analysis using R. During my research I have learnt great tools and techniques from data science that I will like to share. For instance, I have used Regular Expressions (Regex) and Fuzzy Matching to perform string analysis and disambiguate names of authors. I have used data mining by setting up a web driver and control virtual browsers to get data from the internet. I know how to integrate and prepare data for statistical analysis, in particular for econometric application, I hope that you will find my blog useful.
