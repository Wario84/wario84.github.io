About

I'm a PhD candidate of the Programme on Innovation, Economics and Governance for Development (IEGD) at UNU-MERIT. I have developed and implemented algorithms for data cleaning and analysis and I have vast experience using R. I know how to use regular expressions to work with big sets of unstructured raw-data (most common in the industry) for analysis and text mining. I believe that the best way to tackle big data is by efficient programming, parsimony (look for the simplest solution that yields the best result) and parallelization to optimize CPU usage. I have experience working in teams, but I'm a self-learner capable of conducting independent projects and research. I have the great advantage of having experience teaching and tutoring statistics, econometrics and data science in my faculty at the master and doctorate level.
